About Us

Since 2019, The LeoCubs School (TLC) has been a trusted preschool in Amravati, known for nurturing young minds with love, learning, and values. We offer a warm, safe, and engaging environment where children grow joyfully and confidently.

Vision and Mission

Our Vision

At The LeoCubs (TLC) School, we focus on providing a stimulating learning experience to promote every child’s physical, social, and cognitive development.

Our Mission

TLC is committed to providing a conducive environment where children learn through joyful engagement in exhilarating activities. With optimal infrastructure, innovative contemporary educational methodologies, and a dedicated staff, we work in a culture that naturally imbibes values and nurtures strong parent-student-teacher collaboration. We offer a safe, secure, and open learning environment for students from diverse backgrounds and with varying needs.
